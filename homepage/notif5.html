Why we should become a good person?

Quit things that hold you back : Ask yourself, what are the things or habits which are holding you back? We all have some things which we know are stopping us from being our best yet we continue to get back to those things. If you are into such things then you must commit to quit for a lifetime.

Take baby steps: Don’t aim to go from being broke to millionaire, from being fat to being ripped, form being jobless to being a CEO. That never happens and setting such goals will only disappoint you. Instead set so small goals that are quickly and easily achievable, for example if you are broke then have a goal of making just $1 this day, if you are overweight then focus on loosing 1KG in this month.

When you achieve that goal, just make gradual increments until you reach your big goal. Its even better if you can set daily goals. Get rid of those limiting beliefs: Just because you failed or scored less in an exam does not mean that you are destined to fail. Just because she/he said no for your proposal does not mean you should stop finding love. Understand that things don’t always go well, however that does not mean you should not keep trying.

Don’t be a slave to your own mind: Many people are a slave of their own mind, they stop themselves from doing things they enjoy. For example, many people want to go out in the public but they are too mentally scared about being social, they think as if the external world is out to eat them. This is one example of enslaving your own mind and people have enslaved their minds in one or the other way. The best way to break out of this is to do things which you fear the most, of course the things which don’t harm you.

No matter how f***ed up your life is, you have the power to change it: This might sound like a line from the self-help books but this is very true. If you are in the worst phase of your life, I want you to realise that no matter how impossible it might seem, you can get out of the situation provided that you are willing to. There is a light at the end of the tunnel. However that does not mean you can just sit around and expect things to change. You do need to take action to improve your situation and it does get better with time if you do so.

Focus on your own s**t: YouTube wants you to spend time with them, Quora wants you to keep reading, writers like me want you to read my content. While that is ok but you must limit the time and energy you spend on learning from other people. You shouldn’t always keep collecting knowledge but instead you must focus on your own goals and divert your energy there.

Things take time: You wont be your best the other day, the other month or even the other year. Being the best requires a lot of patience and is a gradual process. If you observe nature, it’s massive and beautiful. However also take a note how slowly anything in the nature grows, it takes months and years for a seed to transform into a fully grown tree. But you will also observe that the growth is continuous, the trees do not stop their growth even for a second then why should you?
